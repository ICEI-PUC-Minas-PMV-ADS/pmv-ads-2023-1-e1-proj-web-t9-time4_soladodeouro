Light sky blue appears on mudguards, eyestays, collar flaps and heel pieces, all atop a crisp white on toeboxes, quarters and throats. Black is the final color of the trifecta, gracing Swooshes, laces and collars alike. As mentioned above, the material construction is notable: each “University Blue” piece uses a supple suede, a vast divergence from the AJ1’s standard full leather construction. A black ball-and-wings logo on the collar flap and a blue Nike Air hit on the tongue tag add further branded flair, and the look is completed by a white midsole and “University Blue” outsole.
